## HONEST REVENUE PROJECTIONS

**Current state (Mar 2026):** ~23 clicks/day, 11K impressions/week

| Timeframe | Traffic Est. | Affiliate Revenue Est. | Assumptions |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|-------------|
| Now | 23 clicks/day | $0-50/mo | Too little traffic for meaningful conversions |
| 6 months | 150-300 clicks/day | $200-800/mo | 2% aff CTR × 3% conversion × $50 avg commission |
| 12 months | 500-1,000 clicks/day | $1,000-4,000/mo | Recurring commissions compounding |
| 18 months | 1,000-3,000 clicks/day | $3,000-15,000/mo | Multiple programs, recurring stack |

**The math:** 1,000 daily visitors × 5% click affiliate link × 3% convert = 1.5 sales/day = 45 sales/month. At $75 average commission (blended) = $3,375/month. Recurring commissions from prior months add 20-40% on top.

**Bottom line:** Sign up for programs NOW so tracking links are in place. Revenue follows traffic, not the other way around. Focus energy on content that drives traffic.
