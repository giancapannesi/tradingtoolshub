## Practical Tips

1. **Disclosure is mandatory** — IBKR and some regulators require explicit placement on every page with affiliate links
2. **Cookie stacking** — on comparison pages, place highest-commission links first. TrendSpider (365-day) and Bulenox (lifetime) are most forgiving
3. **Prop firm seasonal promos** — /specials page is positioned for Black Friday, New Year sales
4. **IBKR Publisher CPC** — paid per click, not conversion. Email publishers@interactivebrokers.com
5. **Sub-affiliate programs** — Trade Ideas pays 5% on sub-affiliate sales
6. **Education monetization** — Warrior Trading courses $997–$5,000, 20% commission = $400–$3,000/month on 2-3 sales
