Refinitiv Eikon (Retired) Overview

Status update: Refinitiv Eikon is no longer available. LSEG withdrew every Eikon variant from sale on January 1, 2024 and ended access for existing customers after June 30, 2025. LSEG Workspace is the replacement product.

Can you still buy Refinitiv Eikon?

No. Searchers still use the Eikon and Thomson Reuters names, but LSEG has retired both the Eikon product and the Refinitiv brand. New buyers should evaluate LSEG Workspace. Existing Eikon bookmarks, documentation and workflows may still appear online, which is why the distinction is easy to miss.

What replaced Eikon?

LSEG Workspace is the current workflow platform for professional market data, news, analytics and collaboration. LSEG says it provides Reuters News, customizable workflows, desktop and browser access, an iOS app, Workspace APIs and Office capabilities. Qualified users may also receive integrated news and commentary from Dow Jones brands.

The product remains aimed at institutions and professional teams rather than ordinary retail traders. It should be compared with Bloomberg Terminal and specialist institutional platforms, not low-cost consumer charting software.

How much does LSEG Workspace cost?

LSEG does not publish a universal list price for Workspace. Pricing is quote-based and can depend on the user’s role, datasets, exchange entitlements, region and required workflows. Historical Eikon price estimates should not be presented as the current Workspace price.

Ask LSEG for an itemized proposal that separates the base product, exchange data, specialist datasets, API access and additional users. This makes competing quotes easier to compare and exposes costs that a single monthly estimate can hide.

What former Eikon users need to migrate

Most users receive a modernized interface and access to the current Workspace ecosystem, but programmatic workflows deserve special attention. LSEG’s developer guidance says some older Eikon COM and Eikon 4 Desktop .NET interfaces require replacement. The Eikon Data API and newer Refinitiv or LSEG data libraries may continue to work in supported Workspace configurations.

Before migration, inventory Excel macros, COM automation, desktop-session scripts, saved screens, custom formulas and downstream systems. Confirm each dependency with the account team rather than assuming a desktop upgrade preserves every integration.

LSEG Workspace versus Bloomberg Terminal

Workspace competes on broad institutional data, Reuters News, research workflows, APIs and integration with office tools. Bloomberg remains especially strong where its terminal network, messaging and established desk workflows are decisive. The better choice depends on the content and collaboration a team actually uses.

See our Bloomberg Terminal versus LSEG Workspace comparison for the direct decision, or review Bloomberg alternatives for lower-cost and more specialized options.

Questions to ask LSEG sales

  • Which real-time exchanges and specialist datasets are included?
  • Are Workspace APIs, desktop sessions and Office integrations included in the quote?
  • Which legacy Eikon scripts or macros require redevelopment?
  • How are additional users, mobile access and redistribution rights priced?
  • What onboarding, migration and support commitments are written into the agreement?

Bottom line

A “Refinitiv Eikon price” search now needs a status answer before a price answer: Eikon is retired, LSEG Workspace replaced it, and current pricing is obtained from sales. Preserve the Eikon name when researching old workflows, but use LSEG Workspace when evaluating a new purchase.
