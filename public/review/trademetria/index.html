Trademetria Overview

Trademetria is a versatile trading journal that caters to traders across all asset classes, from stocks and options to forex, futures, and crypto. The platform emphasizes comprehensive performance tracking with detailed breakdowns by strategy, instrument, time period, and risk metrics.

The free plan allows up to 30 orders per month with a single account, making it a solid entry point for newer traders. The paid tiers unlock unlimited trade imports, multiple account management, and advanced analytics including detailed P&L attribution and risk analysis.

Trademetria's strength lies in its balance of simplicity and depth. The interface is clean and intuitive, but under the hood, there are powerful analytics tools for traders who want to dig deep into their performance data. Auto-import from major brokers keeps the journaling process efficient.
