Alpaca Markets Overview

Alpaca Markets is a commission-free, API-first brokerage designed for developers and algorithmic traders who want to build and deploy automated trading systems. The platform provides comprehensive REST and WebSocket APIs for order execution, account management, market data streaming, and position management across US stocks and cryptocurrency markets.

The API is well-documented with official SDKs for Python, JavaScript, C#, and Go, making it straightforward to integrate into trading bots, backtesting frameworks, and custom applications. Alpaca also offers a paper trading environment that mirrors the live API exactly, allowing developers to test their code without risking real capital. The commission-free model means algo traders are not penalized for high-frequency strategies that generate many small trades.

Alpaca serves both individual algo traders and fintech companies that want to embed brokerage services into their own applications through the Broker API. The platform has become a popular choice in the algo trading community, particularly among Python developers who use it with libraries like Backtrader, Zipline, and custom trading frameworks. The main limitation is market coverage — stocks and crypto only, with no futures or options support.
