TradesViz Overview

TradesViz has earned a loyal following by offering one of the most feature-rich trading journals at an accessible price point. The platform boasts over 100 actionable metrics, advanced visualization tools, and supports every major asset class including stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto.

The free tier is notably generous, providing core journaling and analytics capabilities that many competitors lock behind paywalls. The Pro tier at $19/month adds advanced features like custom dashboards, API access, and enhanced reporting, while the Elite tier at $39/month unlocks everything including priority support.

TradesViz excels at data visualization, offering interactive charts and graphs that help traders identify patterns in their performance. The platform supports import from dozens of brokers and data formats, making migration from other journals straightforward.
