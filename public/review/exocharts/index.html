Exocharts Overview

Exocharts is an order flow charting platform built for futures and cryptocurrency traders who need granular volume analysis. The platform provides footprint charts, volume profiles, depth of market (DOM), and delta analysis tools to help traders understand the buying and selling pressure behind price movements.

The platform covers both traditional futures markets and major crypto exchanges, making it a versatile choice for traders who operate across both asset classes. Exocharts offers multiple footprint visualization modes including bid/ask, delta, and volume at price, along with configurable volume profile overlays.

Exocharts positions itself as a focused order flow tool rather than trying to be a full-featured charting suite. This specialization means the tools it does offer are well-tuned for reading order flow, though traders looking for a broader feature set may need to pair it with another platform.
