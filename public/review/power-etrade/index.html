Power E*TRADE Overview

Power ETRADE is the advanced trading platform from ETRADE (Morgan Stanley), specifically designed for active traders and options traders. Available as both a web-based and mobile application, Power E*TRADE provides institutional-quality tools without requiring any additional subscription or platform fees.

The platform's options capabilities are its strongest suit: live streaming options chains with real-time Greeks, a Strategy Optimizer that suggests optimal strategies based on your market outlook, options Analyzer for complex P&L modeling, and the ability to trade multi-leg strategies with up to 4 legs in a single order. The Snapshot Analysis tool provides at-a-glance risk/reward summaries for any position.

Power ETRADE also includes advanced charting with 100+ technical studies, customizable chart drawing tools, real-time streaming quotes, and integrated news from Reuters and other providers. The paper trading feature (Paper Trading by Power ETRADE) allows new traders to practice strategies with virtual money. The platform is included free with any E*TRADE account — no separate subscription needed, making it one of the best value propositions for options traders.
