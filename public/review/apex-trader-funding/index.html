Apex Trader Funding Overview

Apex Trader Funding is a rapidly growing proprietary trading firm focused exclusively on futures trading. Unlike many prop firms that require multi-phase evaluations, Apex uses a simple one-step evaluation process: meet the profit target within the evaluation period while staying within risk parameters, and you receive a funded account.

Apex offers evaluation accounts ranging from $25,000 to $300,000, with profit targets proportional to account size. The risk rules are considered among the most generous in the prop firm industry — there is no daily drawdown limit (only a trailing threshold), traders can trade during news events, and positions can be held overnight. The profit split is 100% of the first $25,000 in profits, then 90/10 thereafter.

The platform supports trading through NinjaTrader, Tradovate, Rithmic, and WealthCharts. Apex is particularly popular for its frequent promotions (often 50-80% off evaluation fees), reset options, and the ability to run multiple funded accounts simultaneously. The firm has paid out over $300 million to traders since its founding.
