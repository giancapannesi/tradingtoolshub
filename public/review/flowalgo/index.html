FlowAlgo Overview

FlowAlgo is a premium options flow and dark pool monitoring platform designed for serious options traders who need the fastest possible data delivery. The platform tracks unusual options activity, large block trades, and dark pool prints across all US options exchanges in real-time.

FlowAlgo is known for delivering flow data faster than most competitors, with trades appearing on the scanner within seconds of execution. The filtering system allows traders to narrow results by ticker, premium size, trade type (calls/puts), sector, and dozens of other criteria.

At $149/month (or $99/month annually), FlowAlgo is positioned as the premium option in the flow scanner market. The higher price point reflects its speed advantage and data completeness. The platform also offers historical flow data, custom alerts, and a clean interface optimized for real-time monitoring during market hours.
