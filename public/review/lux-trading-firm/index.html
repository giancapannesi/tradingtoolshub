Lux Trading Firm Overview

Lux Trading Firm is a London-based proprietary trading company that provides skilled traders with funded accounts ranging from $10,000 to $2,500,000. The firm differentiates itself by operating as a genuine prop trading house rather than a purely simulated environment, giving funded traders access to institutional-grade infrastructure and real market exposure.

The evaluation process is a straightforward 1-step challenge where traders must hit a 10% profit target while respecting a 5% daily drawdown and 10% maximum drawdown limit. There is no minimum trading day requirement on most tiers, and traders are permitted to use Expert Advisors (EAs), trade during news events, and hold positions over weekends—rules that many competing firms restrict.

Upon passing the evaluation, traders receive a funded account starting with a 75% profit split, which scales upward to 80% or higher as they demonstrate consistent performance under the firm's scaling plan. Payouts are processed on a bi-weekly or monthly basis depending on account size. Supported platforms include MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and cTrader, with access to forex pairs, indices, commodities, metals, and cryptocurrency markets.

Lux Trading Firm appeals particularly to experienced traders who value flexible trading conditions and a transparent path to increased capital. The firm's scaling plan rewards consistency, and their dedicated support team is frequently praised in trader communities. While newer to the market than legacy prop firms like FTMO, Lux has built a solid reputation for fair rules and reliable payouts.
