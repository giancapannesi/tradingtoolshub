ClusterDelta Overview

ClusterDelta provides footprint chart and order flow indicators that work across multiple trading platforms including MetaTrader 4/5, cTrader, NinjaTrader, and their own standalone ClusterDelta application. This multi-platform approach is particularly valuable for forex and futures traders who use MetaTrader — a platform that lacks native order flow tools.

The indicator suite includes footprint charts, volume clusters, delta analysis, cumulative delta, and volume profile tools. The MetaTrader integration is a standout feature since most order flow tools target NinjaTrader or proprietary platforms, leaving MT4/MT5 users with few options. ClusterDelta fills this gap by bringing futures market order flow data into the MetaTrader environment.

Developed by a micro team based in Kyiv, Ukraine, ClusterDelta has been serving the order flow community for over a decade. The platform sources tick data from futures exchanges and displays it within whatever charting platform the trader prefers. The small team size means updates and support may be slower than larger competitors, but the multi-platform flexibility is genuinely useful for traders who do not want to switch from their existing setup.
