Bear Bull Traders Overview

Bear Bull Traders (BBT) is a day trading education community founded by Andrew Aziz, author of the bestselling book 'How to Day Trade for a Living.' The platform emphasizes a structured approach to learning day trading, with progressive education paths and strong community mentorship.

BBT offers multiple membership tiers from a $39 seven-day trial to Elite Monthly at $199/month. The community features live trading rooms where experienced traders call out setups in real-time, mentorship programs, and trading psychology resources that address the emotional challenges of trading.

What sets BBT apart is its emphasis on community and psychology alongside technical education. The mentorship model pairs newer traders with experienced community members, creating accountability and support that self-study courses cannot provide. The platform is more affordable than Warrior Trading while providing comparable education quality.
