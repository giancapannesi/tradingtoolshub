Free Education Should Teach a Process, Not Sell Urgency

Strong free education explains market structure, order types, risk, position sizing, testing, and review. Weak material jumps directly to entries, screenshots, or income claims without showing assumptions and failure conditions.

Use free resources to build vocabulary and a repeatable practice plan. Do not treat popularity, production quality, or a large community as proof that a strategy has positive expectancy.

How to Evaluate a Free Course

  • Is the instructor's identity and experience clear?
  • Are risks, losing periods, and limitations discussed?
  • Can you test the method without buying another product?
  • Does the material lead to exercises, records, and review?

For structured paid programs and mentoring, compare trading education platforms.
