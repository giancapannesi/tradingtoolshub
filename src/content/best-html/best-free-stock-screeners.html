A Free Screener Is Valuable When Its Data Matches the Trading Horizon

Delayed end-of-day fundamentals may be adequate for weekly research but unsuitable for intraday momentum. Before comparing filter counts, determine how current the data is, which exchanges and securities are covered, and whether results can be saved or exported.

Build One Repeatable Screen

  1. Define the setup in measurable terms.
  2. Create the screen and record the resulting universe.
  3. Review false positives and missing candidates.
  4. Upgrade only if real-time data, alerts, backtesting, or exports improve that process.
